Data wrangling or data munging is hte process of cleaning, transforming and organizing a dataset to make it suitable for analysis.
It is the process of convering raw, unstructured data and turn into a form that is suitable for analysis and insights

  Identifying and correcting errors and inconsisties in the data
  Handling missing or incomplete values
  Combining multiple datasets
  Converting the data into a format that suitable for analysis
  Identifying and removing outliers 
  Normalizing the data
  Aggragating the data into useful summary statistics
